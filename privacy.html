OpenClaw — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Effective date: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OpenClaw ("we", "us", "our", "the App", "the Application") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the OpenClaw social media content management platform and its associated services.

1. About OpenClaw

OpenClaw is a web-based social media content management application that enables users to create, manage, and publish content across multiple social media platforms — including but not limited to TikTok, Telegram, Instagram, YouTube, VKontakte, and Threads — from a single dashboard.

2. Information We Collect

When you use OpenClaw, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and login credentials for the OpenClaw platform.
  • Social Media Credentials: OAuth tokens, API keys, and access credentials for connected social media platforms (including TikTok). These are stored securely and used solely to publish content on your behalf.
  • Content Data: Text, images, videos, and other media that you create or upload through OpenClaw for publishing.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with OpenClaw, including publishing history, job logs, and feature usage.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, device information, and error logs collected for debugging and service improvement.

3. How We Use Your Information

OpenClaw uses the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and provide access to the OpenClaw platform.
  • To connect to and publish content on your connected social media accounts (including TikTok) as directed by you.
  • To process, optimize, and format your content for each target platform.
  • To maintain publishing history and job status for your reference.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve the OpenClaw service.

4. TikTok Data

When you connect your TikTok account to OpenClaw, we access and use your TikTok data strictly in accordance with TikTok's Platform Terms and Developer Policies:

  • We request only the permissions necessary for content publishing (video upload and publish, basic user info).
  • TikTok OAuth tokens are stored securely on our servers and are never shared with third parties.
  • We do not collect or store TikTok user data beyond what is required to provide the publishing service.
  • You may revoke OpenClaw's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings.

5. Data Sharing

OpenClaw does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your content is shared only with the social media platforms you have explicitly connected and instructed OpenClaw to publish to. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted storage of OAuth tokens and API credentials.
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

OpenClaw retains your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you delete your account or disconnect a social media platform, associated credentials and data are removed from our systems within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the data OpenClaw holds about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data and account.
  • Disconnect any connected social media account at any time.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.

9. Children's Privacy

OpenClaw is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
